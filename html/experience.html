OUR JOURNEY OF EXCELLENCE

With over two decades in the automotive industry, S4annn Motors has consistently delivered exceptional service, quality vehicles, and innovative solutions to our valued customers. Our years of experience have shaped us into the trusted automotive partner we are today.

2002

The Beginning

S4annn Motors was founded with a simple mission: to provide quality vehicles and honest service to our community. Starting with just 3 employees and a small garage, we began our journey into the automotive world.

Founded First Location
2007

Expansion & Growth

After five successful years, we expanded our operations to include a full-service maintenance facility and increased our inventory to over 50 premium vehicles. Our team grew to 15 dedicated professionals.

Expansion Service Center
2012

Innovation Era

We embraced digital transformation, launching our online vehicle inventory system and becoming one of the first dealerships in the region to offer virtual showroom experiences. This period also marked our entry into the luxury vehicle market.

Digital Innovation Luxury Market
2018

Sustainability Initiative

Recognizing our responsibility to the environment, we launched our sustainability initiative, expanding our inventory to include electric and hybrid vehicles. We also implemented eco-friendly practices across all our operations.

Sustainability EV Market
2022

International Recognition

Our commitment to excellence earned us international recognition with multiple industry awards. We expanded to three locations and established partnerships with leading global automotive manufacturers.

Awards Partnerships

Present Day Excellence

Today, S4annn Motors stands as a beacon of automotive excellence with state-of-the-art facilities, a team of over 50 experts, and a reputation for unparalleled customer service. We continue to evolve and innovate while staying true to our founding principles.
