Our Climate System Service Process

We follow a comprehensive approach to diagnose, maintain, and repair your vehicle's climate control systems for maximum comfort and efficiency.

1

Complete System Diagnostics

Our technicians perform a thorough computerized assessment of your climate control system, measuring refrigerant levels, testing pressure switches, inspecting controls, and evaluating overall performance.

2

Refrigerant & Component Analysis

We precisely measure refrigerant levels and check for leaks using specialized detection equipment. Our process includes inspecting compressors, condensers, evaporators, and heater cores for optimal function.

3

Airflow & Ventilation Check

Our experts inspect all blower motors, cabin air filters, ducts, and vents to ensure proper airflow throughout your vehicle's interior, identifying and resolving any restrictions or blockages.

4

Control System Verification

We test all electronic components, thermostats, blend doors, and actuators that control your vehicle's heating and cooling distribution to ensure accurate temperature regulation and mode selection.

5

Performance Optimization

After completing all necessary repairs or maintenance, we conduct a performance test to verify proper temperature output, system pressure, and overall climate control function to ensure your complete comfort.
